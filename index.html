How do I Activate Disney Plus on Multiple Devices?

Disneyplus.com/begin is the initial gateway for new users to set up their Disney+ account. Which allows users to start their subscription or activate their device. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use the link effectively:

Step 1: Sign Up for Disney Plus

Before you can use Disney+ on multiple devices, you must join a Disney+ subscription.

  1. Go to the Disney Plus website: disneyplus.com/begin
  2. Select "Sign Up Now" or "Start Free Trial".
  3. Choose a subscription plan that suits your needs, such as monthly, yearly, and sometimes bundles (like with Hulu and ESPN+ in some regions).
  4. To create a new account, enter your email address and create a strong password.

    5. Note: Disney Plus will use your email for login and notifications.

  5. Enter payment information: Choose credit/debit card information or another payment method if available.

    6. Note: You may have a free trial, so your card won't be charged immediately, but it will be on file.

  6. Confirm your subscription after entering all the required information.
  7. You can now start watching Disney Plus content by logging in on any compatible device.

    8. Note: Make sure Disney Plus is available in your region, as it may be restricted in some countries.

Step 2: Log in to Disney Plus Account

  1. After creating your account, return to disneyplus.com/begin.
  2. Cick the Login option in the top right corner.
  3. To access your account, use the email address and password associated with the Disney Plus account. You can monitor your account, change payment information, and enable Disney+ on multiple devices.
  4. Then click Continue.

Step 3: Navigate to Disneyplus.com/begin to activate Disney+ on multiple devices

  1. To activate Disney+ on multiple devices, visit the official activation website: Disneyplus.com/begin.
  2. Start a web browser on your phone, computer, or tablet and type the address into your browser.
  3. Press Enter to go to Disneyplus.com/begin.

Step 4: Enter 8-digit activation TV code

  1. Open a new browser tab on your mobile device and go to disneyplus.com/begin

  4. After activating, your device will refresh, and you can begin streaming Disney Plus content!

Step 5: Choose Your Devices

Once you’ve entered your confirmation code for activation, you’ll be required to choose the device you wish to enable Disney+ on. You can turn on Disney+ on multiple devices simultaneously, which lets you watch your favorite shows on all of your screens.

Step 6: Confirm Activation

After you’ve chosen your devices, go through the activation process to complete your configuration. Disney+ will verify the activation code and enable your subscription on the selected devices. You’ll receive an email confirmation to confirm that the activation has been successful.
